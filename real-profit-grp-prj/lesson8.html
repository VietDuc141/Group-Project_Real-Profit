+ 1. What is RSI Indicator ?

- Relative Strength Index (RSI) is an oscillator used to measure the market's momentum between two extremes: Overbought and Oversold conditions.

* RSI Setup: Use a 9-period RSI setting.

+ 2. How to Use RSI in Combination with Trends ?

  • In an Uptrend:

    • Once a price correction zone is identified, look for an Entry Long to follow the uptrend.
    • Pay attention to the RSI dropping below the Oversold zone (30 or lower).
    • This is an excellent signal, significantly increasing the probability of a winning Long trade.

  • In a Downtrend:

    • Once a price correction zone is identified, look for an Entry Short to follow the downtrend.
    • Pay attention to the RSI rising above the Overbought zone (70 or higher).
    • This is a strong signal, improving the odds of a successful Short trade.
