+ 1. THE RIGHT MINDSET FOR TRADING.

- Many people step into trading with thoughts like: making easy money, getting rich quickly, and even quitting their regular jobs to trade full-time.

- This is a serious misconception.

- In trading (and investing), there are two fundamental truths you need to understand: PROFITS ALWAYS COME WITH RISKS.

- While everyone aims for profits and strives to minimize risks, there is no such thing as a 100% win rate.

- The best approach for traders is to focus on “increasing the probability of successful trades.”

- To achieve this, traders need:

  • A clear trading plan.
  • A good trading system.
  • Disciplined risk management.
  • A strong and steady mindset.

+ 2. Right mindset.
  • Learn solid knowledge before starting.
  • Focus on slow, steady earnings.
  • Avoid thinking about getting rich until you’ve gained enough experience.
  • Keep your regular job; trading should initially be a side activity.
+ 3. Wrong mindset.
  • Trading first, learning later.
  • Aiming for quick money (leading to high risks and account blowouts).
  • Rushing into the market without a plan.
  • Quitting all other jobs to trade full-time too early.
