+ 1. THE RIGHT MINDSET FOR TRADING

- Many people step into trading with thoughts like: making easy money, getting rich quickly, and even quitting their regular jobs to trade full-time.

- This is a serious misconception.

- In trading (and investing), there are two fundamental truths you need to understand: PROFITS ALWAYS COME WITH RISKS.

- While everyone aims for profits and strives to minimize risks, there is no such thing as a 100% win rate.

- The best approach for traders is to focus on ‚Äúincreasing the probability of successful trades.‚Äù

- To achieve this, traders need:

  1. A clear trading plan.
  2. A good trading system.
  3. Disciplined risk management.
  4. A strong and steady mindset.

+ 2. RIGHT MINDSET
  1. Learn solid knowledge before starting.
  2. Focus on slow, steady earnings.
  3. Avoid thinking about getting rich until you‚Äôve gained enough experience.
  4. Keep your regular job; trading should initially be a side activity.
+ 3. WRONG MINDSET
  1. Trading first, learning later.
  2. Aiming for quick money (leading to high risks and account blowouts).
  3. Rushing into the market without a plan.
  4. Quitting all other jobs to trade full-time too early.
