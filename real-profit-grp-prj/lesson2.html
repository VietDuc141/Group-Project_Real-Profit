+ 1. What is Japanese Candlesticks ?

- Japanese candlesticks are a tool used in technical analysis to describe price movements of an asset, which can include stocks, forex, or cryptocurrencies (crypto).

- Historically, candlesticks were first used by the Japanese to trade rice. Steve Nison discovered this method while working with brokerage firms in Japan. By using Japanese candlestick charts to monitor rice prices, he achieved better trading results.

- This discovery led to widespread trust in the usefulness of Japanese candlesticks, making them a popular tool in financial trading.

+ 2. Benefits of Understanding Japanese Candlesticks

- Mastering candlestick indicators and patterns in technical analysis can help traders:

  • Reduce losses and improve profit ratios.

  • Identify future price trends, aiding in decision-making for actions like:

    • Whether to buy more.
    • When to take profits.

- For long-term investors, precise entry points may not always be critical. However, Japanese candlestick patterns provide valuable insights into future price trends, enabling them to make informed decisions based on current market conditions.
